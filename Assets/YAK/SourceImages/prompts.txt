# YAK ART PROMPTS — the five style prompts, one per idea category.
#
# Each [block] below is one prompt. An ideas.txt section binds to a block by name
# with a "# @style: <name>" tag, so every idea in that section is generated with
# that prompt. "{idea}" is replaced with the idea text.
#
# [rules] is special: it is appended to EVERY prompt. It carries the technical
# constraints the image→grid converter depends on. Two of them are load-bearing —
# do not remove them:
#   1. NO outline / stroke / drop shadow — an outline smears into a thick dark ring
#      when the image is downscaled to the level grid (this is what wrecked the shark).
#   2. ONE flat solid background color, contrasting, no gradient or texture.
#
# [default] is used by any idea that sits outside a tagged section.
# '#' lines are comments and are ignored.

[rules]
TRUE {grid}x{grid} PIXEL ART: the whole image is a strict {grid}x{grid} grid of large
uniform square pixels, nearest-neighbour upscaled to 1024x1024 so each art pixel is a
big solid square block of one color. Hard aliased square edges. NO anti-aliasing, NO
blur, NO soft or feathered edges, NO smoothing, NO in-between blended pixels: every
single pixel is one of the flat colors, nothing halfway. NO gradients, NO shading, NO
highlights, NO glow, NO dithering, NO noise, NO texture — each region is one perfectly
flat color. Use a STRICT LIMITED PALETTE of at most 6 flat colors in total for the
subject. Fill the ENTIRE background with ONE flat uniform solid color that clearly
contrasts the subject and is not used in the subject — no gradient, no vignette, no
lighting, no texture. NO drop shadow and NO contact shadow under the subject; the
subject must not touch or blend into the background. Do NOT draw ANY outline, border,
stroke or halo around the subject — flat color regions only. Exactly ONE single
connected subject, centered, filling most of the frame — NO floating, detached or
separated elements of any kind (no sparkles, no floating hearts, no stray particles,
no motion lines, no separate props); everything must be one connected shape. The
subject must be ONE bold recognizable object, not a busy scene: few large shapes, big
clear features, generous spacing between them. Keep every feature CHUNKY and at least
2x2 art pixels thick — no thin 1-pixel lines, no tiny details, no fine whiskers. No
text, no frame, no border, no photorealism.

[animals]
{idea} — a cute animal with a funny facial expression, oversized eyes, tiny mouth,
chubby proportions, a playful pose and a big clear emotion (smiling, surprised,
sleepy, grumpy). Wholesome and adorable, an instantly recognizable silhouette, drawn
as a premium pixel-art collectible icon: chunky blocky pixel shapes, crisp hard edges,
big flat solid fill colors.

[objects]
{idea} — an everyday object turned into an adorable living character: a cute face,
tiny eyes, a happy expression and a playful personality. Charming, wholesome and
funny, drawn as a premium pixel-art collectible icon: chunky blocky pixel shapes,
crisp hard edges, big flat solid fill colors.

[hybrids]
{idea} — a whimsical hybrid creature combining two unrelated things into one adorable
design. Funny, unexpected and charming, with a clear readable silhouette, drawn as a
highly collectible pixel-art icon: chunky blocky pixel shapes, crisp hard edges, big
flat solid fill colors.

[fantasy]
{idea} — a tiny fantasy creature from a cozy magical world, with an adorable
expression and a playful personality. Magical and charming, drawn as a premium
pixel-art collectible icon: chunky blocky pixel shapes, crisp hard edges, big flat
solid fill colors.

[funny]
{idea} — a funny miniature character with exaggerated emotion, a silly costume or an
unexpected situation. Cute internet-meme energy, the kind of playful collectible that
makes people smile instantly, drawn as a pixel-art collectible icon: chunky blocky
pixel shapes, crisp hard edges, big flat solid fill colors.

[default]
{idea}, drawn as a premium pixel-art collectible icon. Chunky blocky pixel shapes,
crisp hard edges, big flat solid fill colors, bold instantly recognizable silhouette.
Give it real personality: oversized expressive eyes, a clear readable emotion, a
playful pose; charming, wholesome and funny. Skip the face only where it would look
odd (plain geometric objects, scenery).
